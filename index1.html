I am an Economist, a Statistician, and an Information Systems specialist.

Combining my rigorous training in Economics, Statistics, Information Systems, and coupling with my strong quantitative and programming expertise (from my Engineering background), I enjoy finding market-based solutions to problems. I develop data-driven models that incorporate theory, use large data sets, and employ numerical and mathematical techniques to find realistic solutions to problems. I like to align all of my models to answer critical policy questions and assist the stakeholders while designing, debating, and deploying any policy.

Besides developing models, I enjoy searching for unique data and applying statistical learning methods by training machine learning models to answer fun questions. I also enjoy working with diverse data, finding patterns, and developing interactive data visualization applications to communicate with anyone.

I enjoy physical activities when I am not developing models or working with different data. I am an avid gym-goer and like to lift weights. I like climbing indoors and outdoors. I've climbed at Joshua Tree National Park in California, Fern in Arkansas, and Devil's Lake in Wisconsin. I would love to climb in Utah and Colorado sometime soon. I enjoy hiking and fun outdoor activities in general. Lately, I have been practicing indoor time trial cycling, I am enjoying it so far.
